About DGR

It all began as an idea and a vision to provide solutions to different areas of real estate services sometime in 2018. DGR Real estate investments Limited was established and has transformed itself into a full branded, organized and recognized real estate company in Nigeria with it’s unique business model covering wider range of real estate services like, property brokerage, management, investments and development. DGR has effortlessly strived to be an outstanding company providing perfect solutions in all areas of it's services. Each section of it’s services comes with unique business model supported with professional teams who are expert and competent to provide clients with unthinkable and excellent services that ultimately convert transactions into relationship.

DGR real estate aims to be the leading property company in Nigeria providing real solutions in the real estate sector. our business affiliations and partnership with numerous business entity was an idea to put together all what it needs to serve our esteemed clients with the best services that well address their issues and meet their needs and promote the integrity of the company.
