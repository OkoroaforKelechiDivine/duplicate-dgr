Primerose Development Company

Primrose Development Company (PDC) is a property development company whose roots as real estate developers stretch back over thirty years to its progenitor organizations, Primrose Properties Investment Limited (PPIL) and City Securities Limited.

Collectively, the Primrose group of companies have developed and own a property portfolio in excess of $150 million in Lagos, Nigeria.

To better edge its competitors in the market with the integration of the development and construction disciplines. PDC offers a unique and unprecedented value proposition covering all the core skills and requirements fundamental to the success of a real estate venture.

These Include:

• Proven execution capability with over 30 years of real estate development experience in Nigeria.
• Construction and engineering capability introducing international best practices and standards.
• World class management team and partners with a balanced mix of local and international experience.
• Deep established relationships with key players in the real estate sector including consultants, contractors and state authorities.
• Readily available access to funding and capacity to take on large scale projects.
• Committed to the betterment and welfare of communities we do business in.
